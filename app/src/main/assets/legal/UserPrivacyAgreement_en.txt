### 2. Privacy Information and Permission Usage
We understand the importance of personal information to you and will do our best to protect your personal information security. We do not collect your browsing history, bookmarks, passwords, or other personal data. To implement core functions and improve user experience, the browser may request or use certain device permissions. Here is an explanation of the purposes of relevant permissions and the information that may be collected:

1. **Storage Permission**: Used to save downloaded files, web cache, and your local configuration information.
2. **Network Permission**: Used to load webpages, update components, and communicate with the remote AI server you autonomously configure.
3. **Location Permission**: The browser does not obtain your location by default. Geographic location is only provided when a website requests it and you explicitly authorize it.
4. **Camera/Microphone**: Requested only when you actively use the QR code scanning or web audio/video calling features, and will not be accessed in the background.
5. **Device Information and Diagnostic Data**: When the browser crashes or technical errors occur, we may collect necessary device information (such as device model, system version, application version number) and error logs to identify and fix problems. This information is only used to improve browser stability and will not be associated with your personal identity. If you submit a crash report through official channels, the processing of relevant data will follow this agreement.
6. **Remote AI Connection**: If you configure a custom AI server, data will be sent to that server. Olsc does not access such data; please refer to the privacy policy of your chosen AI service provider.
